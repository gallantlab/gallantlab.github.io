We have created a new <a href="https://gallantlab.org/viewer-shortclips-group/">brain viewer</a> that provides a way to inspect cortical visual-semantic conceptual maps at the group level, vertex-by-vertex.

The data for this viewer were generated by pooling visual semantic maps from 15 separate participants who viewed several hours of short movie clips.
