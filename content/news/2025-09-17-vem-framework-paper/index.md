Our latest review paper on the Voxelwise Encoding Model (VEM) framework from <a href="/people/#matteo-visconti-di-oleggio-castello">Matteo Visconti di Oleggio Castello</a> and <a href="/people/#fatma-deniz">Fatma Deniz</a> is now available as a preprint on <a href="https://www.psyarxiv.com/nt2jq">PsyArXiv</a>.

This paper provides the first comprehensive guide for creating encoding models with fMRI data, and complements our VEM tutorials.
