Pycortex, our awesome brain viewer software, has a major new feature!

Thanks to the hard work of <a href="/people/#matteo-visconti-di-oleggio-castello">Dr. Matteo Visconti di Oleggio Castello</a> and Aditya Vaidya, Pycortex now supports headless WebGL viewers! You can create 3D inflated plots without needing a display, and in Jupyter notebooks! See the <a href="https://gallantlab.org/pycortex/auto_examples/webgl/plot_panels_headless.html">documentation here</a>.
