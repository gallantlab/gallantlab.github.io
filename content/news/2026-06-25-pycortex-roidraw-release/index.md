Pycortex, our awesome brain viewer software, has another new feature!

You can now draw regions of interest directly in any pycortex WebGL brain viewer. Switch to <em>Draw</em> mode, lasso an area on the flattened cortical surface, and the stroke is fitted to a smooth, editable outline that you can name, export to JSON, and re-import across viewers. The drawing tool ships as a single self-contained script — <a href="https://github.com/gallantlab/pycortex-roidraw">pycortex-roidraw</a> — that can be dropped into any pycortex viewer. Try it out in our updated <a href="https://gallantlab.org/viewer-stories-group-roidraw/">group-based language comprehension semantic map viewer</a>.
