summary = """Natural navigation requires close coordination of perception, planning, and motor actions. We used fMRI to record brain activity while participants performed a taxi driver task in VR, then fit high-dimensional voxelwise encoding models to the data. Navigation is supported by a network of 11 functionally distinct cortical regions that transform perceptual inputs through decision-making processes to produce action outputs."""
