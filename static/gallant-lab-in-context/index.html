About this figure

Each row is a research theme; a paper's color marks its theme and its horizontal position marks its year of publication (1955–2025).

Ringed, labeled nodes are Gallant Lab papers — the spine that traces each theme's lineage; larger rings mark landmark papers. Small faded dots are related work from the broader field, shown for context.

How to use it
